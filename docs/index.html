GDS Foundation

GDS (Gravitational Dynamic System) is a nonlinear gravitational-dynamic framework inspired by the extreme stability behavior of stellar fusion cores and high-energy cosmic environments.

High-precision numerical conservation tests reached approximately 10⁻¹⁵ relative precision, motivating the development of GDS as a stability-centered dynamical framework.

GSC-X translates these principles into a symbiotic executive stabilization architecture combining predictive instability scanning, adaptive decision logic, nonlinear stabilization, and controlled recovery behavior.
